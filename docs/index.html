At a glance

Who

Ahmed Baki, Tyler Baldeviso, Yuliang Dong, Shuo Li, Vinh Pham.

What

A top-down shoot 'em up drawing inspiration from Space Invaders and Asteroids with a unique twist. Game development (ECS 189) final project.

Where

University of California, Davis.
